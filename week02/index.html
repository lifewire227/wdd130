Anambra, Nigeria

Anambra is a state in southeastern Nigeria. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse festivals, and bustling commercial activities. The state is home to the famous Onitsha Market, one of the largest markets in West Africa. Anambra is also renowned for its educational institutions and vibrant music scene.
